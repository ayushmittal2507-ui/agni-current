About Nidhi

Your partner in evidence-based wellness

Hello, I'm Nidhi Singhal — a Consultant Dietitian and Certified Diabetes Educator with over 8 years of experience helping people navigate the complexities of metabolic health, weight management, and clinical nutrition.

I believe that food is deeply personal. My approach is rooted in listening first — understanding your lifestyle, your preferences, your medical history — and then building a roadmap that is realistic, sustainable, and joyful. No fad diets. No guilt. Just real, compassionate science.

  • M.Sc. in Clinical Nutrition & Dietetics
  • Certified Diabetes Educator (CDE) — NDEP
  • Member, Indian Dietetics Association (IDA)
  • Specialisation in Therapeutic Nutrition & Meal Planning
  • Serving patients across Delhi NCR, virtually and in-person
My Services

Tailored care for every journey

Each programme is designed around your unique health goals — not a template, but a living plan that evolves with you.

🩸

Diabetes Education & Management

Comprehensive support for Type 1, Type 2, and gestational diabetes — from meal timing and carbohydrate counting to understanding your medications and labs.

  • Personalised carbohydrate meal plans
  • Blood glucose monitoring guidance
  • Glycaemic index coaching
  • Ongoing HbA1c tracking support
⚖️

Weight & Metabolic Management

Sustainable, science-based strategies to reach and maintain a healthy weight — with full respect for your body, culture, and food preferences.

  • Caloric & macro-nutrient planning
  • Gut health & digestive support
  • PCOS, thyroid, & hormonal nutrition
  • Mindful eating & habit coaching
🥦

Clinical & Therapeutic Nutrition

Medical nutrition therapy for complex conditions — kidney disease, liver disorders, cardiovascular health, oncology support, and post-surgical recovery.

  • Renal & cardiac diet planning
  • Liver disease nutritional support
  • Paediatric & elderly nutrition
  • Pre & post-operative care plans
